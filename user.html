4. User guide¶

A variety of electronic structure methods are implemented in the PySCF package, including mean-field theories, many-body perturbation theories, and coupled-cluster theory, etc. Many of them also support solid-state calculations with periodic boundary conditions considered. This chapter introduces the basic theories of these methods and some of their most common input parameters.
